Leisure & Custom Tours Across Florida

Discover the Sunshine State your way — personalized itineraries, hidden gems, and the luxury of zero logistics.

Florida is more than beaches and theme parks — and the best way to experience it is from the back seat of a luxury vehicle with a local expert behind the wheel. LuxRide designs personalized tours and leisure transportation around your interests, your pace, and your curiosity. Whether you're spending a day exploring Ybor City or traversing the state from Tampa to Key West, every mile is yours to enjoy.

Our Leisure & Tour Services

Explore Florida Your Way

From local gems to statewide adventures, every experience is tailored to your interests.

Custom Tampa Bay Tours

Ybor City, the St. Pete Pier, Clearwater Beach, local museums, craft breweries, or your own curated route. Your chauffeur doubles as your personal concierge.

Florida Theme Park Transfers

Comfortable, air-conditioned rides to Disney World, Universal Studios, SeaWorld, Busch Gardens, and LEGOLAND. Arrive refreshed instead of road-weary.

City-to-City Travel

Premium long-distance transportation between Tampa Bay and Miami, Orlando, Fort Lauderdale, Naples, Jacksonville, and anywhere in between.

Beach Excursions

Shopping Trips

Historical & Cultural Tours

Visit the Dali Museum, Ringling Museum, St. Augustine, Kennedy Space Center, and Florida's rich collection of landmarks.
