Cruise Port Transfers

Luxury Cruise Port Transfers in Tampa Bay & Florida

Begin your voyage the moment you step into our vehicle. Premium chauffeur service to every major Florida cruise terminal.

Your cruise vacation deserves a beginning as exceptional as the journey itself. LuxRide provides refined, punctual transportation to and from Port Tampa Bay, Port Canaveral, PortMiami, Port Everglades, and every major cruise terminal in Florida. We handle the heavy lifting — literally — so you can start relaxing from your front door.

Our Ports

Cruise Port Transfer Services

Luxury transportation to every major cruise departure point in Florida.

Port Tampa Bay

Dedicated luxury transportation for passengers embarking from or returning to Tampa Bay's cruise terminal. Timely drop-off ensures you never miss embarkation.

Port Canaveral (Orlando)

Comfortable transfers from any Orlando-area hotel, airport, or residence to Port Canaveral's cruise terminals.

PortMiami

Premium service to the world's busiest cruise port, from Miami International Airport, South Beach hotels, or anywhere in the region.

Port Everglades (Fort Lauderdale)

Seamless connections between Fort Lauderdale-Hollywood International Airport, local hotels, and Port Everglades terminals.

Port of Jacksonville (JAXPORT)

Reliable transportation for passengers cruising from Northeast Florida.
